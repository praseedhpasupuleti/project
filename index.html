About:

Usha Rama College of Engineering and Technology, established by Usha Rama Educational Academy, with the aim of inculcating quality education to students and produce young technocrats having good knowledge. The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated to JNTU-Kakinada. It is sponsored by Chicago based Multi Million Dollar Advansoft group.

Advansoft Group made an extensive study of the functioning of premier educational institutions in India and abroad and had an extensive consultation with Industry, academicians and scholars, the efforts of which finally culminated in laying the foundation for the college in 2008. Even before the college commenced its functioning, an elaborate plan was drawn up for acclimatizing and absorbing the students predominantly hailing from a rural background, into an environment demanding learning of frontier technologies.

The Trust comprises a team of highly respected professionals in the field of Academics, Management and Industry, dedicated to creating an Institution imparting quality education. Usha Rama College of Engineering and Technology Trust is well supported by a governing body and an academic advisory council consisting of eminent academicians and professors with decades of experience in premier Institutions.

URCE has within a short span of time emerged as one of the premier professional Institutions in this region. The Institute maintains high standards of education by providing a wide array of world-class academic facilities, employing highly qualified and experienced faculty members and creating an ambience that is conducive to quality education. Departments:

    Polytechnic Courses:

    As per the norms laid by AICTE, college offers the following three year Diploma Engineering courses.
  • Civil Engineering (CIVIL)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (MECH)

    Under Graduate Courses:

    Following are the departments for B Tech course.

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electronics & Communications Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Science & Humanities

FACILITIES

Library:

College has air conditioned central library with Digital facility. It has huge volumes of books, magazines, national, international journals and also CD'S & DVD's .It has reference and periodical section. It consists standard text books by reputed authors. It has separate rooms for group discussion and research work. It has seating capacity of more than hundred.Books are the best means to enhance the knowledge of the individual. Keeping this in view we maintain a spacious library with more than 11,000 volumes, 2,442 Titles, 65 National & International Journals and 897 Compact Disks.


Computer Lab:

The lab is equipped with SIX DELL servers and Four Hundread clients. All application and system software are official versions. Internet connectivity is provided to the campus by 25MBPS EXCEL BroadBand Dedicated line. All the systems in the campus are connected to the server.


Hostel:

Separate hostels are provided for boys and girls. Hygenic food is provided to the students. CC cameras have been installed in the hostel corridors. Attached washrooms are provided in the girls hostel. All the necessary facilities are provided to make them feel like home.

    Hostels are located within the college premesis.

RESULTS

Training & Placement:

USHA RAMA College of Engineering & Technology, established a well structured Training and Placement cell which is an integral part of the Institution. The institute has complete infrastructure for effective functioning of the Training and Placement cell. Training activities are carried out throughout the year at regular intervals. The Training and Placement cell aims at improving the scope of the student towards becoming a corporate citizen. It prepares the students for the campus selections prospectively. It is committed to enrich the student's potential to find employment in the renowned companies.

Our T&P cell is headed by an experienced Training and Placement Officer and is well supported by both faculties as well as student placement coordinators from all the departments. The main objective of T&P cell is to assist the students by arranging pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, personal interviews, Role-plays, JAM sessions, industrial visits, training for soft skills etc.

As a prime activity, the placement cell coordinates quite well with the corporate sector in convincing them for frequent visits to the campus. Confirmations have been received for renowned companies & industries of repute across the country to visit our institute for the campus recruitment programs. Since its inception it started acting as a bridge between industry and academia.

Training and Placement Cell Goals and Objectives :

The college has established Training and Placement Cell, Counseling and guiding of students, industrial training during vacation, organizing campus interviews, providing job market information to students and necessary inputs to face the interviews, organizing Guest Lectures and Campus Selections, arranging industrial training for students, arranging industrial internships for faculty are some of the activities of the Training & Placement Cell. Organize training programs on all the upcoming trends and technologies related placement activity. Training programs are held in three Tyre architecture to bridge the skill gap of students,. The College has a separate Training and Placement Cell, which invites various companies in all branches of Engineering.

The college has established a separate department to take care of the certified courses of IBM, Microsoft, Intel Inside, TATA Technologies, SAP. Every year 500 students will be made part in certified course training Program.

Functions of the T&P Cell :

  • Interaction between industry and institute
  • Campus Recruitment Training to students
  • Arranging Campus Interviews to students
  • Guest lecturers by eminent industry experts
  • Faculty training in the industry
  • Entrepreneurship Development Program
  • Counseling and Personality Development
  • Conducting Mock Interviews and Group Discussions

Also our T&P Cell acted as a directing element for our campus's well-developed infrastructure in facilitating the campus selection programs. The cell maintains a very cordial relationship with all the recruiting industries and also prepares the candidates who are recruited to face the challenges in the corporate world. Apart from this the training and placement cell invites industry professionals to interact with students in order to identify the emerging opportunities in the world of commerce.

The mission of the Training and Placement cell at USHA RAMA is to provide placement opportunities & higher educational guidance to the students in line with their competencies and to help them in finding the best fit at the industry as per their interest & skills.

The Management is providing all the necessary skills for getting a job from 3rd year.

The students are under going rigorous training on aptitude, technical skills and soft skills for a total of 4 weeks in a year.

2 continuous weeks training in first semester and 2 continuous weeks training in second semester.

The above training will definitely help them in getting a good job.
